Evergreen Health Clinic Database

The Evergreen Health Clinic database displays the management of healthcare records, encompassing clinics, patients, doctors, diagnoses, and procedures.

Clinics' details, patients' identities, and contact information are documented, with connections facilitating efficient healthcare services.

Doctors are associated with patients through a many-to-many relationship, while diagnoses and procedures are linked to patient records, enabling specific treatment plans.

This structured design ensures comprehensive healthcare management, increasing patient care within the clinic.
